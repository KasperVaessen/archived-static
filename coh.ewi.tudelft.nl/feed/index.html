On Monday June 5th, CoH organises an activity for alumni and master students: the speed date event. The idea is that alumni can talk in an informal setting with master students of Applied Mathematics and Computer Science about their careers after graduation, their current jobs and the company they work for, in short one-on-one conversations of 15-20 minutes. The event takes place from 18:00-21:30 in the /Pub. Afterwards there will be drinks to further talk about career opportunities. Registration deadline: the registration closes on June 2nd at 23.59h in order to make a rotation schedule for the speed date event.

—– Schedule – June 5th —–

18:00 – 18:30 – Entrance

18:30 – 19:30 – Dinner (free)

19:30 – 20:30 – Speed dates

20:30 – 21:30 – Drinks

We hope to see you at the speed date event!

On Thursday, September 1st the annual alumni barbecue of Constantijn Huygens will take place! Before the barbecue, Kees Vuik, who recently received a royal award, will give a presentation of about half an hour about the current state of affairs at the TU Delft within Mathematics and Computer Science and his research. This presentation is optional. So it is also possible to only join the barbecue.

The presentation, barbecue, and drinks during the evening are all free of charge for alumni!
Registration closes when the maximum number of participants is reached, so register quickly if you want to be sure of a spot! Sign up by filling in the following form:

To discover more about his work and develop your understanding of the dynamics within sustainable projects, join the CoH at this year’s On Stage Interview!
